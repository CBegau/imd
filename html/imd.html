Disclaimer

IMD is still being developed. The developement version is in imd-snapshot, which is not available to the public. The official releases of IMD should work reliably, although we do not guarantee anything. Routines in imd-snapshot may work more or less, or may be broken completely.

There is also a part named "unsupported". It contains routines that have been replaced by better ones or features that will not be continued. These routines may also be defunct.

Authors

IMD was originally developed by Jörg Stadler in the framework of subproject A2 "Molekulardynamik von Quasikristallen" of the Sonderforschungsbereich 382 "Verfahren und Algorithmen zur Simulation physikalischer Prozesse auf Hochleistungsrechnern".

Jörg Stadler left the ITAP in summer 1997. Further contributors have been Marco Brunelli, Dietmar Bunz, Martin Hohl, Christof Horn, Jutta Kaiser, Ralf Mikulla, Matthias Müller and Dieter Wolfangel.

The development of IMD is continued by a team including Erik Bitzek, Franz Gähler, Christopher Kohler, Johannes Roth, Christoph Rudhart, Gunther Schaaf.

The program is in use at the MPI and the MPA.
