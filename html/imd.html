Authors

IMD was originally developed by Jörg Stadler in the framework of subproject C14 "Molekulardynamik von Quasikristallen" of the Sonderforschungsbereich 382 "Verfahren und Algorithmen zur Simulation physikalischer Prozesse auf Hochleistungsrechnern".

Jörg Stadler left the ITAP in summer 1997. Further contributors have been Marco Brunelli, Dietmar Bunz, Martin Hohl, Christof Horn, Jutta Kaiser, Ralf Mikulla, Matthias Müller, Dieter Wolfangel, and Gunther Schaaf.

The development of IMD is continued by a team including Erik Bitzek, Franz Gähler, Christopher Kohler, Johannes Roth, and Christoph Rudhart.

The program is also used at MPI für Metallforschung Stuttgart, MPA Stuttgart, and at the University of Karlsruhe.
